# MoonTale Legal Launch Checklist

Use this internal checklist before publishing or relying on the public legal pages.

- [ ] Confirm Google Analytics retention setting.
- [ ] Confirm Google Analytics data-sharing settings.
- [ ] Confirm Formspree account settings and retention behavior.
- [ ] Confirm the email provider used for `contact@moontaleapp.com`.
- [ ] Enable MFA on GitHub, Google, domain, email and Formspree where available.
- [ ] Establish a privacy-request log for access, correction, deletion, objection and consent-withdrawal requests.
- [ ] Establish an unsubscribe suppression list.
- [ ] Review postal-address obligations before promotional email campaigns.
- [ ] Confirm product-update email consent text and unsubscribe handling.
- [ ] Confirm no child nickname, age, interests, story settings or generated story are sent to Formspree.
- [ ] Confirm Google Analytics events do not include child nickname, story text, interests, email or full form contents.
- [ ] Confirm the browser-data delete control works on desktop and mobile browsers.
- [ ] Confirm the Cookie Settings control works on every public page.
- [ ] Obtain specialist legal review before direct child interaction, payments, school use or a native app.
- [ ] Update the public policies when providers, storage behavior, analytics settings or product behavior change.

## Future Review Triggers

- Direct child interaction in the United States.
- Payments, subscriptions, automatic renewal or paid digital services.
- School or classroom use.
- Native mobile app release.
- Public profiles, comments, messaging, sharing or public user-generated content.
- External AI API or AI provider integration.
- Advertising, targeted advertising, social tracking pixels or session replay.
