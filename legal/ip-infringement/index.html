How to send a report

Send copyright, trademark, text, image, illustration, audio, logo, or other protected-material concerns to contact@moontaleapp.com.

Please include:

  • your name and contact information;
  • identification of the copyrighted, trademarked, or other protected work;
  • the exact URL or location of the disputed material;
  • an explanation of the concern;
  • evidence of ownership or authority where available;
  • a good-faith statement;
  • an accuracy statement.

How MoonTale may respond

MoonTale may investigate, request clarification, temporarily remove or disable material, correct attribution, restore material where appropriate, and preserve information where legally necessary.

MoonTale does not claim to have a registered U.S. DMCA agent.
