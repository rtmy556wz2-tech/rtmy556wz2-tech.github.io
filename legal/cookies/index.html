2. Optional Google Analytics

MoonTale uses Google Analytics measurement ID G-716GP23C93 only after optional analytics consent. Visitors can reject analytics, and rejection does not prevent use of the core prototype. Consent can be changed later through Cookie Settings.

MoonTale does not intentionally transmit child nickname, story text, interests, or story-profile fields to Google Analytics. Analytics events are limited to broad information such as page type, interface language, and general CTA location.

MoonTale does not claim exact Google Analytics cookie durations or retention settings until those settings are verified in the Google Analytics dashboard.

3. Technologies not intentionally used

MoonTale currently does not intentionally use targeted advertising, behavioural advertising, advertising pixels, session replay, browser fingerprinting, precise geolocation, or social-media tracking pixels.
